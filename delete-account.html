Account Deletion Request

Users can delete their account directly from inside the mobile application.

How to delete your account:

  1. Open the application
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Press "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Once the request is confirmed, all personal information including profile data, history, and user content will be permanently deleted from our systems.

Some data may be retained when required for legal, fraud prevention, or security reasons.
