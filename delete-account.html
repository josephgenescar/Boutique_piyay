Account Deletion Request

At RIVAYO REWARDS, we respect your privacy. Users can delete their account directly from inside the mobile application at any time.

What data will be deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your email address and profile information
  • Your reward balance and points history
  • Your ad watch history and game progress

How to delete your data

Option 1: In-App (Recommended)

  1. Open the RIVAYO REWARDS app
  2. Log in to your account
  3. On the Home Screen, tap the Trash Icon (Delete Account) at the top right
  4. Enter your password to confirm
  5. Your account and all data will be deleted instantly

Option 2: Online Request

If you no longer have the app installed, email us at:

josephgenescar@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • The email address associated with your account

We will process your request within 48 hours.
