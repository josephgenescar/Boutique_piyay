# Privacy Policy for RIVAYO-EARN

**Last Updated: February 3, 2026**

Rivayo Tech Entreprise ("we", "us", or "our") operates the RIVAYO-EARN mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

---

### 1. Information Collection and Use
We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

#### Types of Data Collected:
* **Personal Data:** While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). This includes:
    * Email address
    * First name and last name (via Google Sign-In)
* **Device Identifiers:** We collect your device's Advertising ID (AAID/IDFA) to deliver personalized advertisements and track reward eligibility for our "Earn" features.
* **Usage Data:** We may collect information on how the Service is accessed and used (e.g., time spent in the app, features used).

### 2. Use of Data
RIVAYO-EARN uses the collected data for various purposes:
* To provide and maintain the Service.
* To manage your user account and track your rewards/earnings.
* To provide customer care and support.
* To monitor the usage of the Service and detect fraudulent activity or cheating.
* To provide you with personalized advertisements via third-party ad networks.

We will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. 

**To request data deletion:** Please email us at **josephgenescar@gmail.com** with the subject "Data Deletion Request".

### 5. Security of Data
The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard security measures via Firebase to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

### 6. Children's Privacy
Our Service is intended for users who are at least **18 years old**. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If we discover a child under 13 has provided us with data, we will delete it immediately.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
