Account Deletion

Users can delete their account directly inside the application.

Steps:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete my account"
  4. Confirm

When the account is deleted, all personal data such as profile information, learning progress, and history are permanently removed from our servers.

Some information may be kept if required for legal or security purposes.
